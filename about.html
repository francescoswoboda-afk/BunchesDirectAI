Our story

Direct from the farm. Delivered with precision.

Bunches Direct was created to connect the finest rose farms in Ecuador directly with customers across Europe.

What We Do

We specialize in importing premium quality roses, carefully selected at origin and transported under strict conditions to guarantee freshness, consistency, and long vase life.

Our goal is simple. To deliver roses that arrive exactly as expected, fresh, reliable, and ready for market.

By working closely with trusted growers and managing the logistics chain from farm to destination, we reduce unnecessary intermediaries and ensure better quality control at every stage.

We do not just supply flowers. We build long term relationships with our clients by understanding their needs and providing consistent and dependable service.

With a focus on efficiency, transparency, and product excellence, Bunches Direct is a partner you can rely on for high quality Ecuadorian roses across Europe.

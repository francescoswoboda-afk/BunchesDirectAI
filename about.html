Our story

Flowers with Personality, Service with Care

Bunches Direct began as a small weekend market stand and grew into a trusted local flower partner for homes, businesses, and events.

What We Believe

We believe flowers should feel personal, seasonal, and never generic. Every arrangement is handcrafted with movement, texture, and color in mind so that no two bunches feel exactly the same.

By working directly with regional growers, we reduce transit time and bring you fresher stems that last longer in the vase.
